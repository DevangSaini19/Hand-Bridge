## ⚠️ IMPORTANT RULES FOR ACCURATE RECOGNITION

### ✅ DO THIS:
1. **Hold steady for 1-2 seconds** - Don't move your hand
2. **Keep hand in center of camera** - Full hand visible
3. **Good lighting** - Make sure camera can see clearly
4. **Make clear shapes** - Exaggerate finger positions
5. **One gesture at a time** - Don't rush
6. **Wait 2 seconds between gestures** - Let system reset

### ❌ DON'T DO THIS:
1. **Don't move quickly** - Causes wrong detection
2. **Don't partially show hand** - System needs full view
3. **Don't overlap fingers** - Keep them separated
4. **Don't show multiple gestures** - Confuses the system
5. **Don't expect instant results** - Quality needs time

## 🎯 TROUBLESHOOTING WRONG DETECTIONS

### Problem: "Showing Thumbs Up but getting 'Gun'"
**Solution:**
- Make sure ALL four fingers are completely closed
- Only thumb should be visible/extended
- Point thumb directly UP (to ceiling)
- Don't extend index finger at all
- Hold for 2 full seconds

### Problem: "Showing Number 2 but getting 'Peace' or 'Victory'"
**Solution:**
- Keep index and middle fingers VERY CLOSE together
- Fingers should almost touch (< 1cm apart)
- For "Peace", spread fingers 3-5cm apart
- Hold steady for recognition

### Problem: "Showing Number 1 but getting 'X' or wrong output"
**Solution:**
- Point index finger straight up
- Keep palm facing FORWARD to camera
- Make sure all other fingers are curled
- Don't bend index finger

### Problem: "Numbers showing as Letters or vice versa"
**Solution:**
- Palm direction matters! Forward = numbers usually
- Hold position longer (2 seconds minimum)
- Make finger positions more exaggerated
- Check your lighting

### Problem: "Getting random wrong outputs"
**Solution:**
1. **Improve lighting** - Face a window or lamp
2. **Center your hand** - Keep in middle of camera
3. **Hold STEADY** - No movement for 2 seconds
4. **Clear background** - Plain wall behind you
5. **One gesture only** - Don't show multiple signs
