| Number | Hand Configuration | Notes |
|--------|-------------------|-------|
| **0** | Closed fist OR O-shape | 86-90% confidence |
| **1** | Index finger up | 92% confidence |
| **2** | Index+middle up together (or apart for V) | 90% confidence |
| **3** | Index+middle+ring up | 90% confidence |
| **4** | Four fingers up (no thumb) | 92% confidence |
| **5** | All five fingers spread | 94% confidence |
| **6** | Thumb+pinky (Y-shape) | 88% confidence |
| **7** | Thumb+index+middle | 82% confidence |
| **8** | Thumb+4 fingers (no pinky) | 84% confidence |
| **9** | Thumb+index+middle (specific config) | 82% confidence |
| **10** | Two hands, all fingers OR thumb+fist | 88% confidence |

### Extended Numbers
- **11-19**: Combination of 10 + digit (two-hand)
- **20**: Two fists OR special gesture
- **30, 40, 50...90**: Ten multiples (regional variations)
- **100**: Special two-hand gesture

## 🎯 Tips for Best Recognition

### ✅ DO's:
1. **Hold steady** - Keep hand still for 1 second minimum
2. **Good lighting** - Ensure face/hands are well-lit
3. **Clear background** - Plain background helps
4. **Full hand visible** - Keep all fingers in camera view
5. **Proper distance** - 1-2 feet from camera
6. **One sign at a time** - Don't rush

### ❌ DON'Ts:
1. **Don't rush** - Fast movements = low accuracy
2. **Don't blur** - Moving too fast causes motion blur
3. **Don't hide fingers** - Keep hand open and visible
4. **Don't overlap hands** - Unless sign requires two hands
5. **Don't change quickly** - Wait 2 seconds between signs

## 📊 Recognition Priority Levels

The system recognizes gestures in this order:

1. **PRIORITY 1**: Closed fist gestures (A, E, S, 0)
2. **PRIORITY 2**: One finger gestures (1, I, J, X, thumbs)
3. **PRIORITY 3**: Two finger gestures (2, V, U, K, R, Peace, L, Y, Call Me, Rock On)
4. **PRIORITY 4**: Three finger gestures (3, W, F, 7, 8, 9)
5. **PRIORITY 5**: Four finger gestures (4, B, 8, P)
6. **PRIORITY 6**: Five finger gestures (5, O, Hello, Bye, Stop)
7. **PRIORITY 7**: Two hand gestures (10, Praying, Thank You)
8. **PRIORITY 8**: Special letters (G, H, Q, Z)

## 🔍 Troubleshooting Common Issues

### "Not Recognizing My Sign"
- **Check**: Is hand fully visible? Try better lighting
- **Check**: Are you holding steady for 1+ second?
- **Check**: Is palm facing correct direction?
- **Try**: Exaggerate the gesture slightly

### "Wrong Sign Detected"
- **Check**: Are fingers positioned exactly as described?
- **Try**: Make the gesture more distinct
- **Wait**: Let previous gesture clear (2 second gap)

### "Low Confidence"
- **Improve**: Lighting conditions
- **Improve**: Hand stability
- **Improve**: Finger positioning clarity
- **Check**: Distance from camera (too close/far)
